Chapter 1: This Crafty Old Man Is So Bad!

Added 2026-03-19

Evening, Jiangnan City, Xia Country.

Lin Feng stepped out of the flower shop, cradling a vibrant bouquet of roses in his arms.

Today was his girlfriend's birthday.

The flower shop was only about five or six hundred meters away from Jiangnan University, where both he and his girlfriend studied. He began walking toward the campus.

But after only a few steps, he suddenly stumbled over something and almost fell.

"Ouch! That hurts!"

At the same moment, an old man's cry rang out.

Lin Feng looked down and realized he had accidentally stepped on the foot of an elderly man sitting by the roadside.

The old man had white hair and a white beard, his clothes filthy and ragged as if he hadn't bathed in days. In front of him was a stainless steel bowl.

The old man was clutching his cloth-shoed foot, grimacing and groaning in pain.

Embarrassed, Lin Feng quickly apologized again and again, bowing slightly.

He pulled out his wallet — inside were just a few red RMB notes.

He took one out and placed it in the old man's bowl.

After dropping the money, Lin Feng turned, ready to leave.

"Wait, don't go…"

The old man's voice suddenly stopped him.

Lin Feng hesitated, his heart skipping a beat.

Was this old guy trying to scam him?

He was just a poor student himself — his family definitely didn't own any gold mines.

"Sir… is there something else?" Lin Feng asked, his voice trembling slightly.

The old man raised his eyes and said solemnly,

"Young man, I am the 198th-generation heir of the Quanzhen Sect from Mount Zhongnan. I've wandered for many years, but now that I'm old, I wish to return home and rest. Could you spare me another two hundred yuan for travel expenses?"

Lin Feng quietly let out a sigh of relief.

Luckily, it was just two hundred.

If the old man had asked for two hundred thousand, Lin Feng might have had to call the police.

Although Lin Feng wasn’t rich, he had indeed stepped on the old man’s foot just now. Giving a little extra could be seen as covering the cost of medicine.

Swish, swish.

He pulled out another two hundred yuan from his wallet and dropped it into the bowl.

"Two hundred, sir," Lin Feng said, eager to leave before the old man thought up anything else.

But just then, a bony hand shot out and grabbed Lin Feng’s wrist.

It was the old man’s hand!

Lin Feng’s expression changed, and he asked nervously, "S-Sir… what now~~~?"

The old man smiled faintly and said,

"Young man, you are so kind. I have nothing else to give you in return.

Here, take this small box. Inside is a Spirit Pill of Mount Zhongnan, my token of gratitude."

He shoved a small wooden box into Lin Feng’s hand.

"If you take the pill inside, you will become immune to all illnesses. The spiritual energy will cleanse your body, strengthening your flesh, muscles, and bones, and eventually making you a peerless master.

Once your body's meridians are fully opened, you will be able to perceive things others cannot see — the secrets of this world will appear before your very eyes as clear as a mirror."

Lin Feng's mouth twitched violently.

Con artist.

This old guy was definitely a scammer!

A Spirit Pill from Mount Zhongnan?

Take it and become a martial arts master?

Why didn’t he just say he was Wang Chongyang, the founder of the Quanzhen Sect?

Or better yet, a real immortal?

Yeah, right! Like hell I’d believe that!

Seeing Lin Feng's skepticism, the old man continued seriously,

"You don't believe me?

Well, you might not realize it yet, but something major is about to happen to you."

Lin Feng rolled his eyes.

"And what’s about to happen, sir?" he asked sarcastically.

The old man peered at Lin Feng’s head and said gravely,

"Very soon, a vast grassland will sprout above your head."

Lin Feng froze, then grew angry.

"You—you’re cursing me to wear a green hat?!"

(A "green hat" meant being cheated on in Chinese slang.)

How could he not be furious?

This crafty old man was truly rotten to the core!

The old man, however, chuckled.

"I'm not cursing you. Your girlfriend is about to leave you.

But don't worry — when the old leaves, the new will come.

Soon, a major romance awaits you.

Whether it's good fortune or disaster, though, will depend on your fate."

Hearing this, Lin Feng couldn't help but laugh in exasperation.

"Sir, stop joking. My girlfriend and I are perfectly fine. No way we’re breaking up."

As for a "great romance" waiting for him?

Even more ridiculous.

The old man said no more.

He simply let go of Lin Feng’s wrist and waved him off.

"Go quickly. If you don’t hurry, your girlfriend will walk away with some rich young master."

Lin Feng snorted, annoyed.

"Just you wait, old man! I’ll bring my girlfriend back here and show you how great our relationship is!"

Burning with righteous anger, Lin Feng rushed toward Jiangnan University.

He would bring Li Siyu here to show the old man how wrong he was!

This rotten old man, daring to curse me like that! Just wait and see!

(End of Chapter)
